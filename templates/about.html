About

Adesalu Adeola is an indigene of Ekiti State, Nigeria. He attended Jacflorie International, Nursary & Primary school, Ado-Ekiti where He aquired his Primary school Leaving Certificate in the year 2001, he Aaye Oja Grammar School, Aaye Ekiti were he attained his SSCE certificate in the year 2008. He got admitted with merit into The Federal Polytechnic, Ado-Ekiti, Ekiti State to study Computer Science during which he became the head of Institutions cadet corps of Nigeria (ICCON), The Federal Polytechnic, Ado Ekiti Chapter. He Proceeded to Nigerian Television Authority (NTA channel 5, Ado Ekiti) for an intensive Student Industrial work experience scheme (SIWES) Program were he worked as a Audio & Film Editor and Transmission Director. In the year 2011, He was awarded a certificate of National Diploma in Computer Science. He then proceeded to Nigerian Television Authority (NTA channel 5, Ado Ekiti) (Industrial Training) in the year 2012 where worked as Computer Graphics Designer, Camera Man, Audio & Film Editor and Transmission Director. In the year 2015, he was awarded the certificate of Higher National Diploma in Computer Science. He served in 2016 at Alamo Grammar School, Itaogbolu, Akure North L.G.A, Ondo State as a National Youth Service Corps Member. He is currently employed by Broadcasting Service of Ekiti State (EKTV channel 41, Ekiti FM 91.5 Ado Ekiti) for ICT Support, Presenter, Audio & Film Editing, Transmission Directing, Camera and Computer Graphics as a Temporary Staff.
